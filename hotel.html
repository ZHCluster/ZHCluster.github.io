Conference Venue

Zhuhai Holiday Resort Hotel(珠海度假村酒店)
Zhuhai Holiday Resort Hotel is a very nice place with huge gardens near sea shore. LOC will book the standard rooms here for attendees by default. Please tell the LOC clustergalaxy@outlook.com if you would like to book other rooms or choose other hotels.

PRICE: For standard rooms in main building, it is about 600RMB per room per day. Besides, the hotel offers 125m2 villas for families, which could accomodate 4-5 persons with cost of 1900 RMB per day. The hotel also offers economical rooms (shared rooms in villa) down to 400 RMB/day.

TEL:+86-0756-3333838

ADDRESS:No.9 Shi Hua East Road, Jida, Zhuhai, Guangdong, China. 中国广东省珠海市吉大石花东路9号。

If you would like to take public transportation rather than taxi, you have to walk for last few minutes. The map bellow show you the routines from neaby bus station/ship port to the hotel.

Hong-hai-wan Garden(宏海湾花园): Arrivals to Zhuhai Railway Station, Macau Airport , Guangzhou Internationl Airport and Zhuhai Jinwan Airport usually go to Zhuhai Gongbei Customs Bus Station first(see methods here) , then take bus No.9, No.99 from Gongbei Customs Bus Station to here. Then Walk to the hotel along the blue path

Jiuzhou Port harbour(九州港码头): Arrivals to Shenzheng International Airport or Hongkong International Airport could take ships from these airports to Jiuzhou Port harbour. Then Walk to the north gate of hotel along the orange path

Jiuzhou Garden(九洲花园): Arrivals to Hongkong International Airport could also take shuttle bus from airport to Gang-Zhu-Ao Bridge Customs. From Customs, you can take bus No.3 to this bus station. Then Walk to the north gate of hotel along the red path
